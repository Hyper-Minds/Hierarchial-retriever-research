## Court Name:
Supreme Court of India

## Petitioner Name(s):
Mahanadi Coal Fields Ltd. & Anr.

## Respondent Name(s):
Mathias Oram & Ors.

## Case Background: 
The case revolves around the acquisition of land by Mahanadi Coal Fields Ltd. (MCL) for its coal mining operations in various villages of Odisha, resulting in displacement of landowners and their families. The petitioners, Mathias Oram and others, have challenged the compensation and rehabilitation measures provided by MCL.

## Petitioner Arguments:
The petitioners have argued that the compensation and rehabilitation measures provided by MCL are inadequate and do not fully address their concerns. They have also sought directions from the Supreme Court to ensure that the land acquired is developed as per the Third Schedule of the Rehabilitation and Resettlement Policy, 2013.

## Respondent Argument:
MCL has argued that it has already made payments towards compensation for structures and has fulfilled its obligations under the Rehabilitation and Resettlement Policy, 2013. They have also sought to transfer the issue of Method of calculation adopted by the Commission to the High Court of Orissa.

## Issues discussed in Court:
The Supreme Court has discussed various issues including the application of the Gopalpur Model for determining compensation, the eligibility of landowners and their families for employment benefits, the suitability of resettlement sites, and the payment of one-time compensation to displaced families.

## Final Decision:
The Supreme Court has directed that the Commission should complete its task and finalize reports for villages Kiripsira, Ratansara, Jhupuranga, and Tumulia. It has also directed that the High Court shall decide the Writ Petition (C) being No. 39185/2023 regarding the Method of calculation adopted by the Commission.

## Court's Reasoning:
The Supreme Court has relied on its previous judgments and orders to arrive at its decision. It has held that the First Schedule of the Rehabilitation and Resettlement Policy, 2013 is applicable to the acquisition in question, made by MCL, in respect of villages where reports were not approved prior to 28.10.2015.
